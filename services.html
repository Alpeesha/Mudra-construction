Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)

Advanced testing methods to evaluate structural integrity without damage

Precise Evaluation Without Structural Damage

Our Non-Destructive Testing services utilize state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to assess the condition of concrete and structural elements without causing any damage. This allows for accurate evaluation while preserving the integrity of your building.

We employ various NDT methods to provide comprehensive insights into your structure's condition, including specialized tests for carbonation depth, concrete core analysis, and chemical composition assessment.

Rebound Hammer Test

Measures surface hardness to estimate concrete strength

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity

Detects internal flaws and measures concrete quality

Carbonation Depth Testing

Measures carbonation penetration in concrete to assess corrosion risk

Concrete Core Testing

Laboratory analysis of concrete cores for compressive strength

Chemical Analysis

Laboratory testing of concrete and reinforcement materials

Cover Meter Survey

Measures concrete cover and rebar location

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our structural engineering services

How often should a structural audit be conducted?

For buildings older than 30 years, a structural audit should be conducted every 5 years. For buildings between 15-30 years old, an audit every 10 years is recommended. However, if you notice any signs of distress like cracks, sagging, or water leakage, an immediate audit is advised.

What is the typical duration for a structural audit?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of the building. For a typical residential society, the site inspection takes 1-2 days, followed by 5-7 days for analysis and report preparation. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide repair solutions after the audit?

Yes, our comprehensive service includes detailed repair methodology, preparation of BOQ (Bill of Quantity), tender documents, and supervision of repair work. We provide end-to-end solutions from assessment to completion of repairs.

Are your services available throughout Maharashtra?

Yes, we provide our services across Maharashtra with our base in Mumbai. We have successfully completed projects in Pune, Thane, Navi Mumbai, and other major cities in the state.
