Sunshine Housing Society - Comprehensive Structural Audit

This project involved a detailed structural audit of a 30-year-old residential building in Malad East. The building showed signs of structural distress including cracks in beams and columns, spalling concrete, and water leakage issues.

Location: Malad East, Mumbai
Duration: 3 Weeks
Building Age: 30 Years
Units: 40 Flats

Key Findings & Solutions

  • Identified corrosion in reinforcement due to carbonation
  • Recommended concrete repair and anti-corrosion treatment
  • Provided structural stability certificate after repairs
  • Supervised the complete repair work

Green Valley Apartments - Building Repair & Restoration

This comprehensive building repair project addressed multiple structural issues in a 25-year-old residential complex in Goregaon. The project included concrete repair, facade restoration, and waterproofing solutions.

Location: Goregaon, Mumbai
Duration: 8 Weeks
Building Age: 25 Years
Units: 60 Flats

Scope of Work

  • Concrete cancer treatment and repair
  • Structural crack injection with epoxy resins
  • Facade restoration and painting
  • Terrace and balcony waterproofing
  • Quality supervision throughout the project
