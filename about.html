EXPERIENCE: Personal: 12 Years | Company: 4 Years

Mudra Consulting Engineers is a professional civil engineering consultancy firm specializing in Structural Audit, Building Repairs, and Rehabilitation Consultancy. Founded in 2021 by Mr. Sagar Kishor Jadhav, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in structural engineering across Maharashtra.

Our focus is on ensuring structural stability, safety, and durability of existing buildings through scientific assessment, modern repair techniques, and transparent project management. We serve residential housing societies, commercial buildings, industrial premises, and government institutions.

With our headquarters in Mumbai, we have successfully completed numerous projects, helping building owners and residents ensure the safety and longevity of their structures.
