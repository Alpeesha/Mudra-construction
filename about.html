About Mudra Consulting Engineers

With over 12 years of experience, we are committed to delivering technically sound, economical, and long-lasting solutions for aging structures across Mumbai and Maharashtra.

Home / About Us

Professional Engineering Guidance

Mudra Consulting Engineers is a professional civil engineering consultancy firm specializing in Structural Audit, Building Repairs, and Rehabilitation Consultancy. Founded in 2011 by Mr. Sagar Kishor Jadhav, we have grown to become a trusted name in structural engineering across Maharashtra.

Our focus is on ensuring structural stability, safety, and durability of existing buildings through scientific assessment, modern repair techniques, and transparent project management. We serve residential housing societies, commercial buildings, industrial premises, and government institutions.

With our headquarters in Mumbai, we have successfully completed over 150 projects, helping building owners and residents ensure the safety and longevity of their structures.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development over the years

2011

2013

Expanded services to include comprehensive building repair and rehabilitation consultancy, completing our first major project in Malad.

2015

2018

Completed our 50th project and expanded our service area to cover entire Mumbai Metropolitan Region.
