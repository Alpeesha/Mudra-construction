Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our consultation and project initiation process

How quickly can you conduct a structural audit after initial contact?

We typically schedule site visits within 3-5 working days of initial contact. For emergency situations where structural safety is a concern, we can arrange a preliminary assessment within 24 hours.

Do you charge for initial consultation?

We offer a free initial consultation either at our office or via video call to understand your requirements and provide preliminary advice. Detailed site inspections and formal audits are chargeable services.

What areas in Maharashtra do you serve?

We serve the entire state of Maharashtra with our base in Mumbai. We have successfully completed projects in Mumbai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Pune, and other major cities across the state.

What information should I have ready before contacting you?

It's helpful to have basic information about your building such as age, type of construction, specific issues you're facing, and any previous repair history. If available, original building plans can also be useful.
